About Buildforce

Built on trust, driven by craftsmanship

Buildforce is the construction division of Phalendrome. We bring the same commitment to precision and quality that defines our group to every project we build.

Our Story

From one trade to a full-spectrum build team

Buildforce was established to provide comprehensive construction services under one roof. Rather than managing multiple contractors across different trades, clients can come to us for every stage of their build.

With over 18 trades covered in-house, we handle everything from structural groundwork to final finishes. Our team is built on the values of the Phalendrome group: clear communication, honest pricing, and workmanship that lasts.
