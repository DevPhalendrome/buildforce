About Us

Who is Buildforce?

A division of Phalendrone Solutions. We're a team of experienced construction professionals delivering quality across 18+ trades.

Our Story

Built on experience. Driven by quality.

Buildforce was founded as a specialized construction division of Phalendrone Solutions, bringing together decades of combined experience across the full spectrum of building trades.

We exist to fill a gap in the market: a construction partner that offers both depth and breadth. Depth of expertise across individual trades, and breadth across the complete project lifecycle. From the first blueprint to the final coat of paint, we have the skills and equipment to deliver.

Our team includes certified welders, qualified electricians, master carpenters, experienced tilers, and specialists in every trade we offer. Every project is backed by the full weight of our collective expertise.
